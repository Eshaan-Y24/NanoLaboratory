About us

The Nanomaterial Research Laboratory has contributed highly qualified technologists and academicians to R/D institutions and various government institutions. This has been possible through research and development projects in the laboratory. The hard work of the head of the laboratory is reflected in large number of projects. Through these projects, not only the advancement of technology in laboratory has been achieved, but also it has helped the scholars to apply the seeds of research and technology wherever possible.
