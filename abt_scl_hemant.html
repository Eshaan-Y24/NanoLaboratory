Hemant Kumar Arora

Hemant Kumar Arora is currently pursuing Doctor of Philosophy in Engineering Physics under the guidance of Dr. Nitin K. Puri in the Department of Applied Physics, Delhi Technological University, Delhi. With interest in latest technology he is working on different Nanocomposite materials for applications in gas sensors, renewable source of energy and also device fabrication. He did M.Tech in Nano Science and Technology from Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Delhi and B,Tech in Electronics and Communication. He has done project during masters on green energy source like hydroelectric cell.
